Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Egyptian Football Facts handles information when you use our website.

Information we collect

We do not require an account to use the app. We do not collect your name, email, or payment details unless you choose to contact us through the Contact Us form.

Data stored on your device

The app saves some information locally in your browser using localStorage, including:

  • Saved facts and saved videos
  • Video likes and comments you add

This data stays on your device. Clearing your browser data will remove it. We do not receive this information on our servers.

Third-party services

We use API-Football (api-sports.io) to load club, standings, squad, and fixture data. When you use those features, your browser sends requests to their servers. Please review their privacy policy for how they handle data.

Video reels may load from external hosting services. Those providers may collect standard usage data according to their own policies.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The app relies on localStorage for saved content and preferences on your device.

Children

Our app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, visit our Contact Us page.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date below will reflect the latest version.
