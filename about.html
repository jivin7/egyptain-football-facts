Who we are

Egyptian Football Facts is a fan-built web app for lovers of Egyptian football. We bring together club search, World Cup standings, curated facts, football reels, and saved content in one place.

What we offer

  • Live Egypt club data and search powered by API-Football
  • World Cup Group G standings and Egypt knockout path
  • Curated facts about clubs, players, and the national team
  • Football video reels you can like, comment on, share, and save
  • Save your favourite facts and videos on your device

Our mission

We want every Egypt fan to discover something new about the Pharaohs, the Cairo Derby, the Premier League, and Egypt on the world stage. Whether you support Al Ahly, Zamalek, or the national team, this app is for you.

Data sources

Club, squad, standings, and fixture information comes from third-party football APIs. Curated facts are written for education and entertainment. World Cup Group G backup data reflects published final group stage results when live API data is unavailable.
